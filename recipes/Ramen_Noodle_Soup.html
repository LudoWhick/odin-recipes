Ramen Noodle Soup

Description

Ramen Noodle Soup is a popular Japanese dish that consists of wheat noodles in a flavorful broth. It is typically garnished with various toppings such as sliced pork, green onions, and boiled eggs. The broth can be either soy-based or miso-based, and it is known for its rich and savory taste. Ramen Noodle Soup is a comforting and filling meal that is enjoyed by people of all ages. It is a staple in Japanese cuisine and has gained popularity worldwide. Whether it is a quick lunch or a late-night snack, Ramen Noodle Soup is a delicious and satisfying choice.

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon chili oil
  • ½ teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan combine broth and noodles.
  2. Cover and bring to a boil over high heat; stir to break up noodles
  3. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil and ginger.
  4. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green onions.
